Our story

A Symphony of Flavour & Rhythm

At HOC, we believe food is a form of art—each dish telling a story. We've blended the timeless elegance of Indian classical dance with the rich flavors of Indian cuisine to craft an experience that’s as soulful as it is delicious. Each dance form, inspired by the ancient Natya Shastra, reflects mythology, culture, and emotion—just as our dishes express tradition, aroma, and taste.

Indian classical music Indian classical music, encompassing Carnatic (South Indian) and Hindustani (North Indian) styles, is a deeply spiritual and intricateart form based on ragas (melodic frameworks) and talas (rhythmic cycles). Did you know? Indian classical music is around 3000 years old with its origin in Samaveda. The music split into 2 carnatic and Hindustani around 13th century Tansen, a court musician of Akbar, is said to have lit lamps by singing Raga Deepak.
